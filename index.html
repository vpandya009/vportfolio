A little about me

Hi… I am Vagmita Pandya

I'm an who's endlessly curious about how things work …and how they can work better.

Soo I experiment a lot! It's how I learn fast, test ideas, and figure out what actually works for real people. I genuinely love understanding people — what frustrates them, what confuses them, and what would make their lives just a little bit easier. That's the whole point, really.

Oh, and I'm an engineer turned designer — which means I overthink AND make it pretty. The systems brain never left, it just shows up in Figma now. Dangerous combo, honestly.

Outside design? Travelling to places with no plan, adventure activities that sound terrifying until they're not, pottery (yes, really), and books I carry everywhere. Currently trying to get into vlogging — still figuring out which version of me shows up on camera. Scuba diver certified is happening this year. Skydiving is on the list. So are a lot of other things I haven't told anyone yet. Life's too short for boring weekends. 😉
